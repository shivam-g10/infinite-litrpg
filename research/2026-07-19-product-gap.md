What makes this more than generic AI choice fiction?

## Facts

- Daily serialized chapters are established web-novel behavior.
- TaleAI already offers protagonist setup, choices, custom directions, ongoing memory, and generated chapters.
- AI Dungeon, NovelAI, and Sudowrite already maintain structured story context.
- Royal Road permits disclosed AI text but warns against low-effort generation.

## Inference

- Genre toggles, protagonist naming, and three choices are commodity features.
- Strong wedge is independently progressing named actors with private knowledge and deterministic progression state.
- Locked viewpoint turns hidden world simulation into narrative discovery.
- Inspector makes agent architecture visible to judges without leaking it into reader mode.
- Chapter cap creates measurable completion pressure missing from endless chat.

## Unknowns

- Long-run reader retention after novelty.
- Whether structured continuity materially improves enjoyment.
- Whether generated prose quality survives many chapters.

## Decision Impact

- Ship one LitRPG world, not a genre platform.
- Prove world motion, continuity, POV isolation, and convergence.
- Defer public feed, monetization, and broad personalization.
